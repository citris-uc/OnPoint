Setting up OnPoint

We'll help you get setup with OnPoint in just three steps:
  1. Identify your medications,
  2. Create a schedule from your medications,
  3. Use the Timeline to manage your schedule and appointments.

Identifying medications

The first step in the app will be to add medications. We offer multiple ways to identify your medication, including searching by name or searching by barcode.

Scheduling medications

After you've identified all your medication, we will help you schedule the medications so that you can receive timely notifications.

Using the timeline

Once you have a medication schedule setup, your main interaction with the app will be through the timeline. The timeline is a central place for you to view your medication schedule, be notified of what medications you have taken, and be aware of medication you have yet to take. The timeline keeps you on track, on point.
